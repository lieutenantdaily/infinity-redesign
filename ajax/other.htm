Buy, Sell and Rent Textbooks in Huntsville


Infinity College Bookstore is Huntsville’s top choice for textbook deals. We have the lowest prices in Huntsville on textbooks. If you find any local store with a better price, we will match or beat it!

We carry books for UAH undergraduate classes and some Calhoun classes. For all other schools, we can order new or used textbooks for you. Orders usually arrive the next business day, so there’s just a short wait.

Please bring the ISBN numbers for the books you need so we can help you get the right titles. The course number and section is often OK, but ISBN numbers are best. ISBNs are often found on a course syllabus, or the college’s official bookstore website.

We can also rent textbooks. Renting is a really cheap option for textbooks! Here's information about renting textbooks from us.

Infinity is the best place where you can sell textbooks in the Huntsville area. We buy books from all local colleges. We even buy some old editions and books with water damage that are hard to sell! Textbook buybacks are done year-round. Here is more information about our Huntsville buyback program.
