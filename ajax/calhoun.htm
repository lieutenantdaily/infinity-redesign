Calhoun Bookstore in Huntsville


Looking for a Calhoun bookstore in Huntsville? Look no further!

Infinity College Bookstore carries a full inventory of textbooks for Calhoun classes. These books are offered at very substantial discounts.

They are much cheaper than what you’d have to pay on campus at the Calhoun Community College Bookstore.

Infinity is just a couple miles from the Calhoun Campus in Huntsville. Here is a map and directions, plus our store hours.

Infinity carries new and used Calhoun textbooks and has rentals available for most titles. Please bring your class schedule for faster service.

We also buy textbooks from students year-round. Our buyback prices are often the highest in Huntsville. We even buy some old editions and books with water damage that are hard to sell! Learn more about our buyback program.

We cannot currently accept Pell Grants, but we are working on this for next semester.

Though we mainly serve the Huntsville Campus, we can sometimes help students who attend Calhoun in Decatur. If you don’t live near Huntsville, it’s best to give us a call at (256) 513-9181 to make sure we can supply books for you.

When you need Calhoun textbooks or have books to sell, come to Infinity College Bookstore! Here’s a page with directions and a map to the new Calhoun bookstore in Huntsville, AL.
