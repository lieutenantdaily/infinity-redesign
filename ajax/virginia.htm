We Buy Virginia College Textbooks


Looking for a place to sell Virginia College textbooks in Huntsville, AL? Perhaps we can help you.

Infinity College Bookstore buys Virginia College textbooks from students year-round. Students often find that we’re the only local store willing to buy their textbooks.

We even buy some old editions and books with water damage that are hard to sell! Here's more about our buyback program.

Infinity is just a few miles from the Virginia College campus in Huntsville, AL. It’s only a 5-minute drive. The best thing to do is to bring all your books at once, even the ones from prior semesters. Here is a page where you can see a map and directions, plus our store hours.

If you have just a couple of books to sell and would like to save a trip, you can give us a call at (256) 513-9181 to make sure we can buy the books you have. If you have lots of books, it’s best to bring them by and let us price them in person.

So how much money do you get? Well, it varies widely. Lots of students get between $20-40, especially if they have less than ten books. There are quite a few people who “clean out their closets,” bringing 20-30 books, many from previous semesters. These students often receive $100 or more for the books that still have value.
