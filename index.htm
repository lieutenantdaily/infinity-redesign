Infinity College Bookstore is the only off-campus bookstore for The University of Alabama in Huntsville and Calhoun Community College in Huntsville, AL.




Wayne King

manager

sell your books

We buy textbooks from any college. Usually, after final exams, you won’t need a textbook any longer. By selling your books, you get cash for books you no longer need.

We buy textbooks all year round. We have always thought it’s better to buy all the time, rather than doing like some bookstores who only buy around finals.

buy your books

We offer a full inventory of new and used textbooks at unbeatable prices. We stock both UAH and Calhoun Community College textbooks. Most are priced around 10% less than the on-campus stores.
